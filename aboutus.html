About Us



Since inception CBC has always had its own brand of biscuits namely the Devon brand which today is known primarily for its Cream Crackers and Digestives, however, also includes a variety of cream biscuits, cookies, various crackers and a sugar free tea biscuit. Between 1987 and 2004 CBC established distributors in several Middle Eastern markets and it now sells its DEVON branded products in Lebanon, Jordan, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Dubai and Bahrain. CBC exports its products to over 25 countries. In 1986 CBC diversified into snack foods production. Today the snack food factory has 4 lines; a Florigo pellet frying line, two American Extrusion lines making bake and fry type extruded products and a Cretor's heat popped popcorn line. Since joining the European Union CBC has also developed its business into niches of private label particularly for the UK and Irish markets. CBC regularly participates in international exhibitions like PLMA Amsterdam, WABEL Paris, ANUGA Cologne, ISM Cologne and Gulfood Dubai. The Company is BRC certified coupled with a Hazard Analysis Critical Control Point (HACCP) System in place which is internationally recognized as being one of the most effective safety control systems in the food industry.



By putting our customers and their needs first, our mission is to always be leaders in the biscuit and snack food market in Malta, and together with carefully selected distributors we shall compete on the global market trying to satisfy specific consumer requirements. In so doing we take pride in our work and never, and under no circumstances, shall we compromise food safety & quality in the products that we produce.



Consolidated Biscuit Co. Ltd undertakes to ensure the health & safety and welfare of all employees, subcontractors and visitors on its premises and commits itself to achieving this objective by:

-Setting targets for continual improvement.

-Identifying risks and taking necessary action to eliminate or control these risks.

-Complying with all legal requirements relevant to health & safety.

-Promoting best practice in all aspects of health & safety at work.

-Providing the necessary financial, training and advisory resources.

-Promoting employee participation in action groups.



Consolidated Biscuit Co. Ltd commits itself to achieve the objectives set out in the Mission Statement by:

-Ensuring that the statutory requirements and legislation governing food and safety will be the minimum standards adopted by the Company.

-Focusing on customer needs and satisfy their requirements.

-Maintaining and continuously improving the quality and integrity of our products and operations whilst fulfilling our obligation to produce safe, legal and authentic food.

-To maintain hygiene levels which ensure a clean and safe working environment.

-Ensuring that all employees are trained and aware of their responsibilities in meeting the objectives set in the Company's Food Safety & Quality Policy.

-Regularly reviewing, and amending when necessary, the Food Safety & Quality Policy and ensuring all employees are notified of any changes.
